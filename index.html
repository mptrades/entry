// FAQ

Common questions.

Which assets does the indicator work with?

Primarily optimized for MNQ and NQ (Nasdaq Futures). Also works for ES, MES and other futures — tick size and asset are fully adjustable in the menu, custom assets supported.

Which TradingView plan do I need?

The indicator works technically with any TradingView plan. For real-time futures data we recommend at least the Essential plan.

How do I get access after purchase?

After purchase you send me your TradingView username. I manually add you as an invite-only user — the indicator then appears in your library under "Invite-only Scripts". Usually within 24 hours.

How exactly does the limit order entry work?

Once a signal is confirmed — the next candle closes above the RB low — the indicator draws a line at the open of the rejection candle. That is your limit order level. Place a buy limit there and wait. When price retests the level you fill at the top of the structure with the tightest possible stop and best RR.

What is the difference between Starter and Pro?

The indicator itself is identical in both plans. Pro additionally includes direct Telegram access where I explain setups live every session, post my own trades, and do weekly chart reviews.

Which timeframe works best?

Optimized for 1-minute and 2-minute charts during the NY session (9:30–16:00 ET). All time windows are fully adjustable in the settings menu.
